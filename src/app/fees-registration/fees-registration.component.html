Registration

1. All existing B.Tech. students must report to IIIT Kota office for registration from July 17 to July 18, 2017. Course registration form can be obtained from the office. Classes will start from July 19, 2017.

2. All existing B. Tech. students should pay fees before registration using State Bank Collect online portal. Select Internet Banking or SBI debit card while paying fee through SBI Collect for lesser charges. Students are advised to retain their receipt generated through State Bank Collect for proof of fee deposited. Do not use cash / direct account transfer mode for fee payment.

Only those students, having an educational loan, should deposit Demand Draft to SBI MNIT Campus Branch along with SBI Collect online challan (select SBI Branch for payment through DD). Demand Draft, if any, should be drawn in favor of INDIAN INSTITUTE OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY KOTA , payable at Jaipur. Any charges levied by banks for fee payments, will be paid by student .

Important: Existing students admitted in session 2016-17, should produce fresh income certificate for family income from all sources. Income certificate must be signed by the competent authority as prescribed in valid income certificate authority document. Failure to produce this income certificate by registration date, will result in fee payment calculated as per highest fee bracket.

3. A late fee of 1000/- will be charged if Institute fee for the semester is not paid in full even after two days from registration date. Also, students should obtain written permission from Coordinator, IIIT Kota if institute fee is not paid by July 24, 2017.

4. 4. All new entrants through CSAB/JoSAA-2017 – registration will be as per JoSAA schedule – from July 22 to July 26, 2017. All the admitted students need to report to Office of IIIT Kota, 2nd Floor, Prabha Bhawan, MNIT Jaipur Campus for their course registration.
