  1. 1) 4-year B. Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering (student strength 60)
  2. 2) 4-year B. Tech. in Electronics and Communications Engineering (student strength 60)

Accomodations

The students admitted to B. Tech. program of IIIT Kota may be accommodated in the hostel premises of MNIT Jaipur as per the availability of hostel rooms. The final decision on the availability of accommodation (for both boys and girls) and on the revision of hostel fee,lies with the mentor institute MNIT Jaipur.
